PigeonShip's Ingenuity

The idea of PigeonShip sprung upon Jared after finishing a rough semi-pro golf tournament. To add further insult, in the midst of his three-hour drive home he realized he’d forgotten his gear. That’s when the ingenuity of PigeonShip struck him – how nice it would be if another driver along his same route could pick up and drop off his golf gear!


Over the next couple years, Jared organized the team, technology, and crowd-sourced couriers to bring to fruition PigeonShip. Now businesses can ship almost anything, anytime for less money and with on-demand delivery flexibility!
